# Security Policy

## Our Commitment

We take the security of our systems, software, and data seriously. We actively monitor, assess, and improve our security posture to protect our customers, partners, and internal operations.

## Reporting a Vulnerability

If you discover a security vulnerability, please report it responsibly and privately.

Do **not** open public issues or pull requests for security concerns.

### Preferred Reporting Method

Use GitHub's private vulnerability reporting:

1. Navigate to the affected repository
2. Click on the **Security** tab
3. Select **Report a vulnerability**

### Alternative Contact

If private reporting is not available, contact us at:

**support@syntechtix.com**  

## What to Include

To help us respond quickly, please include:

- Product the vulnerability was found it
- Product version
- Description of the vulnerability
- Steps to reproduce (if applicable)
- Impact assessment
- Suggested remediation (if known)

## Response Timeline

- Acknowledgement: within 2 business days  
- Initial assessment: within 5 business days  
- Ongoing updates provided as appropriate

## Disclosure Policy

- We investigate all valid reports promptly
- We coordinate fixes before public disclosure
- We request that you do not disclose the issue publicly until it is resolved
- We may acknowledge contributors unless anonymity is requested
